Stats

  • The All Blacks have over a 77% win rate since they played their first game in 1903.
  • The All Blacks have a winning record against every team they have ever played.
  • The All Blacks haven't lost at Eden Park, their home ground, since 1994.
  • The All Blacks have been the #1 ranked team in the world longer than every other country combined, including a streak of 10 years straight from 2009-2019.
  • The All Blacks have won the Rugby World Cup 3 times in 1987, 2011, and 2015.
  • The All Blacks have held the Bledisloe Cup since 2003.
